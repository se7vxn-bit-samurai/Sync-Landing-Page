How it works

Three steps. Zero setup.

No installation, no account, no config. Open the page and go.

01

Drop your schedule

Drag an Excel or CSV roster export onto the page. The parser reads patterns, identifies names and dates, and builds the model automatically.

02

Explore and manage

Navigate by calendar, table, cards, or planner. Check coverage, review anomalies, record swaps, add notes, and plan future months.

03

Export and share

Generate styled Excel workbooks or image snapshots. Everything stays local — your data never touches a server.
